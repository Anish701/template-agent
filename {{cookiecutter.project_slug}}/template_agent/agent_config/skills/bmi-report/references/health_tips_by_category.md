# Category-Specific Health Tips

## Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

**Focus:** Healthy weight gain through nutrient-dense foods

- Eat more frequently — 5-6 smaller meals throughout the day
- Choose nutrient-dense, calorie-rich foods (nuts, avocados, whole grains)
- Add healthy fats to meals (olive oil, nut butters)
- Include protein with every meal to build muscle mass
- Strength training to build muscle alongside increased calorie intake
- Consult a healthcare provider to rule out underlying conditions

**Avoid:** Empty calories, skipping meals, excessive cardio without proper nutrition

## Normal (BMI 18.5–24.9)

**Focus:** Maintaining healthy weight and overall wellness

- Continue balanced nutrition with variety of whole foods
- Maintain regular physical activity (150 minutes moderate exercise/week)
- Stay hydrated (8+ glasses of water daily)
- Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours per night)
- Manage stress through mindfulness or relaxation techniques
- Regular health check-ups to monitor wellness

**Avoid:** Complacency — healthy habits must be maintained

## Overweight (BMI 25–29.9)

**Focus:** Gradual weight loss through sustainable lifestyle changes

- Aim for 0.5–1 kg weight loss per week (sustainable rate)
- Reduce portion sizes and track calorie intake
- Increase physical activity to 300 minutes moderate exercise/week
- Replace refined carbs with whole grains, vegetables, lean proteins
- Limit added sugars and processed foods
- Focus on building long-term habits, not quick fixes

**Avoid:** Crash diets, extreme restrictions, unsustainable exercise routines

## Obese (BMI ≥ 30)

**Focus:** Comprehensive lifestyle modification with professional support

- Consult healthcare provider or registered dietitian for personalized plan
- Start with small, achievable goals (e.g., 10-minute walks daily)
- Track food intake to identify patterns and areas for improvement
- Build a support system (friends, family, support groups)
- Address emotional eating triggers and develop coping strategies
- Consider medical supervision for weight management program
- Celebrate non-scale victories (energy levels, mobility, mood)

**Avoid:** Self-blame, extreme diets, isolation

## General Wellness Tips (All Categories)

- **Hydration:** Drink water throughout the day
- **Sleep:** 7-9 hours per night
- **Stress management:** Practice mindfulness, meditation, or yoga
- **Social connection:** Maintain supportive relationships
- **Regular check-ups:** Monitor health markers beyond BMI
